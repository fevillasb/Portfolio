Self-Balancing Inverted Pendulum

Fully 3D-printed two-wheel inverted pendulum platform with closed-loop PID control via IMU-based tilt estimation. Characterized transient response through step-disturbance testing, tuning control gains to minimize overshoot and settling time.

  • PID Control
  • IMU
  • C++
  • Control Systems

Designed a 3D-printed excavator-style payload system in Fusion 360 with two actuated joints (elbow, wrist). Created custom printed bearings and gears; integrated high-torque servos with bearing-supported pivot points to reduce friction and enable precise object manipulation.
